Document Viewer is a fork of the last GPL version of EBookDroid.
We need your support to fix outstanding bugs, join development on our project site!
Project site

Currently, Document Viewer supports the following formats:
  • PDF
  • DjVu
  • XPS (OpenXPS)
  • Comic Books (cbz) (NO support for cbr (rar compressed))
  • FictionBook (fb2)
